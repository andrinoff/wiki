# Project Governance
Oreon development is guided by the maintainers and the current project leader.

The project leader is responsible for the overall direction of the project and helps coordinate development, releases, and long term goals for Oreon.

## Current Project Leader
The Oreon project was started in January 2024 by **Brandon Lester**, who currently serves as the project's leader.

The project leader helps guide the vision of Oreon, coordinates development priorities, and works with contributors and maintainers to ensure the project continues moving forward.

## Maintainers
Maintainers are responsible for the day to day development of Oreon.

Maintainers may:

- review and merge contributions
- request revisions to proposed work
- reject changes that do not align with project goals
- revert changes that introduce problems
- assist with release preparation and project infrastructure

Maintainers help ensure the stability, quality, and long term sustainability of the project.

## Project leadership changes
Oreon is intended to remain a long term community project.

Over time, project leadership may rotate. If a leadership change becomes necessary, a new project leader may be elected from trusted contributors and maintainers within the Oreon project.

The goal of leadership rotation is to ensure the project can continue to grow and remain stable even as contributors change over time.

## Role of BoostyConnect Technologies
BoostyConnect Technologies is the legal holder of Oreon and provides infrastructure support & organizational oversight for parts of Oreon.

This may include helping secure infrastructure, services, and other operational resources used by the project.

BoostyConnect does not generally direct the technical vision or day to day development of Oreon. The project's direction remains primarily guided by the project leader and maintainers.
